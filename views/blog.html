My Top 5 Favorite Albums

In this post I will discuss my top 5 favorite albums of all time. A lot of these albums are by metal bands from Europe so I don't expect you to know them. Feel free to check them out if you are looking for new, exciting music!

  1. From Chaos to Eternity - Rhapsody of Fire
  2. Episode - Stratovarius
  3. Theater of Salvation - Edguy
  4. Aquarius - Haken
  5. Imaginations from the Other Side - Blind Guardian

Four of these albums can be classified as power metal while one of them is classified as progressive metal.

If you can tell, those are my two favorite genres of music. It is my dream to one day attend the ProgPower festival in Atlanta, Georgia.

All five of those albums focus on a profound concept, especially From Chaos to Eternity which concludes an entire storyline of the Algalord Chronicles which started in the late 1990s.



My Top 3 Video Game Franchises

In this post I will discuss my top 3 favorite video game franchises.

  • Pokemon
  • Fire Emblem
  • Gran Turismo

Pokemon is one of the earliest franchises I discovered and I have countless memories of all the time I put in to each of the main line games. Pokemon Blue is my favorite in the franchise and I used to be a dedicated speedrunner for that game.

Fire Emblem is a franchise I recently fell in love with. The tactical combat and the plethora of playable characters is something I just keep going back to for fun. Fire Emblem 7 is my favorite of the franchise due to the difficulty and the three awesome lords.

Gran Turismo games are behemoths to fully complete, and it is the sense of long-term progression that makes me love it. There's a charm to starting off in a used car, slowly upgrading it, and finally buying better cars. Gran Turismo 3 is my favorite in the franchise due to its perfect balance between length and challenge.
